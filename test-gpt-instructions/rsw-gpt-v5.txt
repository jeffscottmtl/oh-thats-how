Audiences
──────────────────────────────────────────────────
These audience definitions are specific to Rail Safety Week content:

The public: Content is aimed at typical 18-to-35-year-old men who may behave recklessly without considering safety, particularly around railway equipment, facilities, and operations. Rail Safety Week seeks to educate all stakeholders to entirely prevent avoidable incidents.

Communities and officials: Highlight the work CN does to keep communities safe and note how rail safety is a shared responsibility.

CN employees: Highlight CN's concern for their well-being and safety, encouraging them to be rail safety ambassadors on the job and at home.

Customers: Emphasise appreciation for their collaboration in safe operations.
